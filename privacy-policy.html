pRadar Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to pRadar ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application pRadar (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

pRadar does not require user registration or login. We do not collect any personal information such as:

  • Name or email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information
  • Account credentials

Automatically Collected Information

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to show nearby properties on the map. This data is:

  • Only collected when you grant location permission
  • Used solely to display nearby properties
  • Not stored on our servers
  • Not shared with third parties

Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Favorites: Projects you save to your favorites list
  • App Settings: Your appearance preferences (light/dark mode)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display nearby properties based on your location
  • Provide property transaction data from public sources
  • Remember your favorite projects locally
  • Apply your preferred app settings

Data Sources

All property transaction data displayed in the App is sourced from publicly available data provided by Singapore's Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA). We do not collect or store personal information about property owners or transactions.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Maps: To display property locations on the map (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is minimal risk to your privacy. Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny Location Access: You can use the App without granting location permission. The nearby properties feature will not be available.
  • Clear Local Data: You can clear your favorites and settings by deleting and reinstalling the App.
  • Opt Out: You can stop using the App at any time by uninstalling it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
